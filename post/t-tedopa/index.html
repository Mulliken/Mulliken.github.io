Simulating Finite-temperature Open Quantum Systems with Matrix Product States

\@Cheet on 2021/01/01

In this post we consider a special type of open quantum system where the “system” part is coupled to a bosonic bath. The general form of the Hamiltonian of the open quantum systems is \[ H = H_{sys} + A_{sys}\otimes \int_{x_0}^{x_1}(a_x^\dagger + a_x)g(x)\,\mathrm{d}x + \int_{x_0}^{x_1}a_x^\dagger a_xh(x)\,\mathrm{d}x \]
